Shrimp Scampi /w Linguine

Description:

A seafood dish made of shrimp cooked in butter and garlic, and served with linguine pasta. 20 minutes of preptime and 20 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of linguine pasta
  • 4 tbs butter
  • 4 tbs olive oil
  • 1 lb of shrimp (peeled)
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 shallots (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the linguine in the water for 6-8 minutes (drain after)
  3. Melt 2 tbs of butter with 2 tbs of olive oil in a skillet
  4. Cook and stir shallots and garlic in the butter and oil mixture for 3-4 minutes
  5. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper; add to skillet and cook until pink while stirring occasionally (remove shrimp when cooked)
  6. Pour white whine into skillet and bring to a boil
  7. Melt 2 tbs of butter and add olive oil into the mixture
  8. Toss the linguine and shrimp into the mixture until it is all coated
