Pasta Salad

Description:

The Recipe for our Pasta Salad is very simple and should only take 20 minutes to prep and 10 minutes to cook it. 6 servings should be able to be made from our recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 Bottle of Dressing (Italian Dressing)
  • 6 tbs of seasoning Mix
  • 2 cups cherry Tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 Can of Black Olives
  • Three Chopped Bell Peppers (Green, Yellow, Red)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water and cook the pasta in the water for 10-12 minutes (rinse and drain after)
  3. Whisk the Dressing with the Seasoning Mix until smooth
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the vegetables in a salad bowl
  5. Pour the seasoned dressing over the salad and toss the salad
