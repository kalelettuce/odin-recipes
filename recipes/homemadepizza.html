Pasta Salad

Description:

A simple homemade pizza crust recipe that takes 10 minutes of prep and 15 minutes to cook. 20 minutes of additional time might be needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Pizza pan
  • Whatever ingredients you want in your pizza!

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients; Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let is stand until its creamy (10 minutes)
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture and beat until smooth.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough out onto a flour surface and roll into a 12 inch circle
  6. Transfer the dough to the pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings you chose
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown
